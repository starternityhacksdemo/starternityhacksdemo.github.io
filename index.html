About The Event

Technical Hackathon - Introducing Starternity Hacks

For Internity Foundation, we exist to create an ecosystem of innovation and contribution. Since we started this initiative, we aimed at fulfilling this purpose, and we are doing internships, technical events, and meetups. And we see hackathon as a major event to inspire young minds and to imbibe a culture of creation in them, where they are solving pressing issues of our society using technology and continuously improving those solutions too. To accomplish this, we wanted to specialize ourselves in executing hackathons, we started with sponsoring a hackathon in JIIT Noida, it was a successful event for us, and we could see our potential to step in as an organizer. We took the next step in executing a remote hackathon in the midterm of our 45 Days Summer Internship and did it 3 times, successfully. After gaining experience from the above, we believe it is the right time to organize an offline technical hackathon and this will be a leap in our crusade of creating the Internity Ecosystem.

Proposed Themes for Starternity Hacks 1.0

We have three proposed theme for the event  -  Theme for this Hackathon will be - “AGRIM BHARAT”: Advanced India India, where you live and contribute towards growth is on the other hand is getting affected by many of the acts which are unknowingly done or ignored with the passage of time. But it is never too late to invent, innovate and create a new India for yourself, others and the upcoming generation for a clean, digitize and healthy India. In this age of entrepreneurship, there are startups that take care of basic needs such as food, clothes, and a roof for shelter. Let’s come together and make India more advanced. We know that the hard thing is done by figuring out how to start. But it is not impossible. We can make it!

Swachh Bharat

The old saying “Cleanliness is next to godliness” is hardly audible to Indian ears. India has been blessed in many ways. We are one of the oldest civilizations on the planet. The combination of a rich philosophical and scientific tradition as well as a renowned religious history makes India more beautiful. It’s our responsibility to maintain this admirably of India. But still, problems are prevailing in the society and harming the environment. Urban areas of India generate 1, 88,500 tonnes of municipal solid waste (68.8 million tonnes per year) and waste generation increases by 50 percent every year. More than 80 percent of this waste reaches open dumpsites causing public health issues, environmental degradation, and resultant climate change. Plastic and e-waste form the major chunk of this waste, with minimal facilities to take care of such environment degrading substances. Can you seriously imagine how India is dealing with such a massive problem? So, what are we waiting for? The energetic youth generation can bring a lot of change. Yes! You need to find a solution to this problem because you are a citizen of this country. Take this problem as your own and get set to find the solution, bring innovative ideas in consonance with the ambition of Swachh Bharat.

Digital India

“I dream of a Digital India where access to information knows no barrier.”

- Shri Narendra Modi

A dream to make India digitalized so that if you are in any corner of India, you can access information, pay bills and many more. It’s not easy to fulfill a dream. It contains a lot of obstacles. The dream of digital India is not that easy. It’s too facing a lot of challenges like poor connectivity, Digital divide, slow infrastructure development, regulatory roadblock, threatening issues of cyber-attacks and leaks.
You must be aware of 9 Pillars of Digital India:

  • Broadband Highways
  • Universal Access to Mobile Connectivity
  • Public Internet Access Programme
  • E-Governance: Reforming Government through technology
  • e-Kranti – Electronic Delivery of Services
  • Information for All
  • Electronics Manufacturing
  • IT for Jobs
  • Early Harvest Programmes.
Digital India is achievable but it has a set of challenges. Youth with problem-solving mind can definitely solve this. Bring innovative ideas to make our India more Digitalized.

Smart Lifestyle

Making life easy is all we want. Having healthy food, being a fitness freak or getting Medicare solutions on time is all we want. But hold on friends! This is still a challenge for our country. one of the most critical needs today is access to good healthcare. Billions around the world, particularly people in the India subcontinent, struggle because they don’t get proper access to healthcare. Even those who have access have a sour experience. We have apps that could order food while sitting at home, book tickets online and even book a cab. However, finding doctors is still unbelievably tough Patient records are either maintained manually or if it online stored, are often not accessible or understandable. The pharmacy apps are also available but still face delivery issues. It’s important to get rid of this problem. Find a solution and just out of it.
